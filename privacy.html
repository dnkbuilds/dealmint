Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

DealMint ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily sign up for deal notifications.
  • App usage data — anonymous analytics such as which deals were viewed or tapped, to help us improve the product. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Device information — basic device type and OS version, collected anonymously for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, payment details, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you deal notifications if you have subscribed (email only).
  • To understand how the app and website are being used so we can improve them.
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Amazon Associates Disclosure

DealMint is a participant in the Amazon Associates Programme, an affiliate advertising programme designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.in.

When you click a product link and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This commission does not influence which products we feature.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers. We do not use Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel. The only external requests made are to Google Sheets (to load deal data) and to Amazon (for product images).

5. Data Storage and Security

Email addresses collected via our newsletter signup are stored securely and used only for sending deal notifications. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data. We retain your email only as long as you remain subscribed.

6. Your Rights

  • You can unsubscribe from deal notifications at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email we send.
  • You can request deletion of your email from our list by contacting us.
  • The DealMint app does not require account creation and can be used fully without providing any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

DealMint is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of DealMint after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
