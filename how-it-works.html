How DealMint Works

Our AI-powered system screens thousands of Amazon prices every day to surface only the deals worth your attention.

Our system continuously monitors Amazon India product prices across hundreds of categories. Using AI-based price screening, it identifies price movements and flags products where the current price is significantly below the typical range.

90-Day History

Compare with 90-day price history

Every candidate deal is compared against its 90-day price history. We only consider a deal genuine if the current price is at or near the lowest it has been in the past 90 days — not just lower than a suspicious "MRP" that nobody ever paid.

3
Curated

Show only the best 6 deals

After filtering, we hand-review and select just 6 deals to show at a time. This is intentional — fewer, better deals. No noise, no filler. The list refreshes every 6 hours so there's always something new worth checking.

🤖 AI Price Screening

Our AI model is trained to detect real discounts vs fake ones — it filters out common tricks like inflated MRPs, temporary price hikes before a sale, and products that are "always on sale." What you see are deals that passed a genuine value check.
